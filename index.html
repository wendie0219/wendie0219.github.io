Research

Computer-Assisted Language Learning

With the rapid advancement of technology, Computer-Assisted Language Learning (CALL) has been widely adopted in language education. We employ quantitative approaches to evaluate the effects of AI chatbot-assisted language learning by analyzing language production through a set of multidimensional and fine-grained indices. Our findings offer pedagogical implications for classroom application, and also provide technological directions for the fine-tuning of language-learning models.
